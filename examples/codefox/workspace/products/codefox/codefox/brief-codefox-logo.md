# CodeFox Logo-Led Campaign Brief

CodeFox logo-led campaign visual system. Palette: #F58220 fox orange, #0E2B3A dark teal outline, #FFF3E0 warm cream, #1BBF9C verification mint, #222222 ink. Use the uploaded template at `workspace/products/codefox/codefox/references/codefox-logo-template.png`: a seated orange fox mascot with closed eye, cream tail tip, dark teal outline, and friendly lowercase `codefox` wordmark.

Audience: developers and small teams who want repeatable launch visuals from a locked brand system.

Single job: announce that CodeFox Marketing Harness turns one locked brand style into a complete set of versioned promotional images.

Visual direction:

- Lead with the fox mascot identity, not an abstract SaaS dashboard.
- Use warm cream backgrounds and dark teal structural lines so the orange fox remains the first signal.
- Add a restrained mint token rail to represent verified style consistency.
- Show versioned social cards, posters, and web banners as output artifacts, but keep text minimal and legible.
- Leave safe margins for all headline text; no cropped headline, no tiny faux UI text.
- Do not invent a different animal, do not turn the fox into a realistic photo, and do not add extra unrelated logos.

Signature element: the seated fox watches a locked token rail transform into neatly stacked launch assets.
