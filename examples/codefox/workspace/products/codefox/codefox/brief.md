# CodeFox Marketing Harness Brand Brief

Subject: CodeFox Marketing Harness, a developer-facing system that turns locked brand tokens into versioned campaign image artifacts.

Audience: product engineers, growth teams, and founders who need repeatable launch visuals without letting every project invent its own prompt style.

Single job: make the harness feel like a precise production pipeline, not a generic AI art toy.

Design direction from the local frontend-design skill:

- Palette: #101820 pipeline ink, #2FE6A7 verification mint, #F7F1E5 artifact paper, #FFB000 release amber, #E84A5F alert coral.
- Type: compact technical grotesk for labels and metadata; confident geometric sans for campaign headlines; avoid ornamental display faces.
- Layout: stable editorial grids, visible version lanes, checksum-like ticks, and artifact frames that feel inspectable.
- Signature: a "locked token rail" running through the composition, connecting style tokens on the left to versioned artifacts on the right.
- Texture: crisp vector-like product illustration, subtle paper grain, hard-edged light, no glossy stock-photo atmosphere.

Style critique:

Avoid the usual dark dashboard with random neon blobs. The mint rail must represent verification and continuity, not decoration. The release amber should appear only as a small status signal. Use the paper neutral to keep marketing assets legible across web, social, and poster formats.
