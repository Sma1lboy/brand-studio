# Claude Flag Campaign Brief

Create one promotional visual for Claude by Anthropic, aimed at builders and knowledge workers who want help with difficult problems.

The first image should use a flag as the central object: a modern fabric banner caught in clean light, carrying the word "Claude" with a restrained campaign line. The image should feel like a quiet declaration rather than a loud tech ad.

Design direction:

- Calm, research-led, and precise.
- Warm neutral base with a clay accent and a small cool counter-accent for depth.
- Tactile paper and woven fabric details instead of glossy AI abstraction.
- Sparse but confident typography.
- No fake official announcement badges, fake sponsor logos, or cluttered app UI.

Suggested visible copy:

- "Claude"
- "把复杂问题，交给清晰思考"
- "The AI for problem solvers"
